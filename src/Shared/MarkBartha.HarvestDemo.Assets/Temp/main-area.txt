Integration notes

Open/close menu:

Add hidden â†’ remove it on .backdrop.

Add/remove translate-x-full on .menu-panel to slide.

Mobile add flow: toggle the section[data-state="adding"] hidden/unhidden when the FAB is pressed. Hide it after adding/canceling.

Empty state: hide the list container and unhide the data-state="empty" section when there are zero items.

Accessibility: buttons include aria-label; consider managing aria-expanded and aria-hidden as you toggle states.
